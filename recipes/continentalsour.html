Continental Sour

Description

A Whiskey Sour made 'continental' by the addition of a splash of port. Adapted from a recipe discovered in Mas 2010 at Stagger Lee bar in Berlin, Germany.

Ingredients

  • 2 shot Bourbon Whiskey
  • 1 shot Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 shot Sugar syrup 'rich' (2 sugar to 1 water, 65.0Â°brix)
  • 1 dash Angostura Aromatic Bitters
  • 1/2 shot Pasteurised egg white (or aquafaba)
  • 1/4 shot Tawny Port
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Step 1:SHAKE first 5 ingredients with ice and strain back into shaker.
  2. Step 2:DRY SHAKE (without ice) and fine strain into ice-filled glas.
  3. Step 3:Finish with a drizzle of port around drink.
